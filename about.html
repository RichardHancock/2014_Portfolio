About Me

Hi, I am a Game Programming student enrolled at Bournemouth University.
I have been interested in computers and technology ever since I was a small child, and have been developing my skills ever since.
I've been gaming about as long, starting on the Mega Drive and moving onto PC later on. My favourite genres include: Strategy, RPG and Sandbox.

My first experiences of programming were modifying games or just opening them up to see how they functioned.

I have always enjoyed learning about new technologies and how I can use them to keep adapting my skills with the latest information.

Career Aspirations

To become a Game Programmer in a medium sized company, such as CCP or Team 17. However I would also love to work in a small (indie) studio producing software and/or games.
