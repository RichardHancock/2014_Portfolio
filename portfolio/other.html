Bentley Six Speed - Maya

For this project I was tasked with modelling a Bentley Six Speed. I had never used Maya and found it quite challenging to learn and use.

While the final model is not perfect, I am still pleased with the result as it was a very different skill for me and didn't play into my strengths.

UDK - Seismic Situation

This was my first level in the Unreal Development Kit 3. I had never designed a level before, and found the process fun but challenging.

Trying to create a realistic looking world was especially hard without being able to create custom models/textures, so a lot of creative thinking was needed to keep the illusion alive.

The level was partially based on the Fallout series, mainly for the post-apocalyptic setting.
